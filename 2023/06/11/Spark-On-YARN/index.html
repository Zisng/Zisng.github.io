七、Spark On YARN

1.client模式测试

SPARK_HOME=/export/server/spark ${SPARK_HOME}/bin/spark-submit –master yarn –deploy-mode client –driver-memory 512m –executor-memory 512m –num-executors 1 –total-executor-cores 2 ${SPARK_HOME}/examples/src/main/python/pi.py 10

2.cluster模式测试

${SPARK_HOME}/bin/spark-submit –master yarn –deploy-mode cluster –driver-memory 512m –executor-memory 512m –num-executors 1 –total-executor-cores 2 –conf “spark.pyspark.driver.python=/export/server/anaconda3/bin/python3” –conf “spark.pyspark.python=/export/server/anaconda3/bin/python3” ${SPARK_HOME}/examples/src/main/python/pi.py 10
